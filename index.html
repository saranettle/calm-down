However, if you find yourself worrying constantly when thereâ€™s no apparent cause, you may have an anxiety disorder. Anxiety affects as many as 18% of Americans, and it afflicts women twice as often as men. The sensation of anxiety is often accompanied with muscle tension, shortness of breath, restlessness, and difficulty with concentration. In more serious cases, sufferers of anxiety may experience panic attacks, an extremely heightened sense of urgent anxiety. If you are finding yourself in a state of anxiety, I hope the resources below help you.
